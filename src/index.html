Wire-transfer Euro or CHF to Aragon Smart Contracts

  1. Click button (and do real payment if can choose the red pill)
  2. Onboard & KYC yourself if not done yet
  3. Funds will be sent to Aragon Smart Contract

All transaction are handled within KYC-ed environment which is enforced by smart contracts (ERC20 stablecoins).

Aragon using real money

Aragon contract on mainnet using real (live) payments and KYC.

Make a minimum donation of 5 EUR to Fibree.org. After the payment you NEED TO DO FULL KYC on cash36.io. If done, your payment will converted to our stablecoin and sent to the contract. Check arrival of your donation on Aragon or etherscan
Based on the transaction-hash, the contracts can send back funds to you.
