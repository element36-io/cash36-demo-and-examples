Wire-transfer Euro or CHF to Aragon Smart Contracts

  1. Do payment
  2. Onboard & KYC yourself if not done yet
  3. Funds will be sent to Aragon Smart Contract
  4. (Target contract may trigger wire-transfers)

All transaction are handled within KYC-ed environment which is enforced by smart contracts (ERC20 stablecoins).

Aragon using real money

Aragon contract on mainnet using real (live) payments and KYC.

After the payment you NEED TO DO FULL KYC on cash36.io. If done, your payment will converted to our stablecoin and sent to the contract. Based on the transaction-hash, the contracts can send back funds to you.

Check out the Aragon contract on Aragon.org.
